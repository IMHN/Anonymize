Anonymize your links!

A blank referrer increases privacy. Webmasters can use this Anonymize tool to prevent their site from appearing in the server logs of referred pages as referrer. The operators of the referred pages cannot see where their visitors come from any more.

Example:

https://imhn.github.io/Anonymize?https://pages.github.com

Usage: https://imhn.github.io/Anonymize?your-url
